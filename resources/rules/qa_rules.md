# One Piece TCG — General Rules Q&A

## Basic Rules

| Question | Answer |
|---|---|
| Can I place my deck and reveal my Leader card in any order? | No. After you and your opponent place your decks, you will then reveal your Leader cards to each other. |
| Are there any rules regarding the order in which Life cards are placed? | Yes. Place the card at the top of your deck so that it is at the bottom of your Life cards. |
| What happens to a Character when its power is reduced to 0 or less by an effect? | The Character remains on the field with a power of 0 or less. |
| Can I transfer a DON!! card already given to a Character to another Character during the Main Phase? | No, you cannot. |
| Can I play another Stage card if there is already a Stage card on my field? | Yes. You must reveal the Stage card being played, pay the cost, trash the card already in the Stage area, and then place the new Stage card in the Stage area. |
| Is there a limit to the number of cards I can have in my hand? | No, there is not. |
| What happens if an effect that gives −10000 power is activated against a Character with 6000 power? | The Character's power becomes −4000. |
| What happens if 1 DON!! card is given to a Character that currently has −4000 power? | The Character's power becomes −3000. |
| Can a Character's cost be less than 0? | No. When a Character's cost falls below 0, it is treated as 0. |
| If a Character with negative cost is returned to hand, can I play it at negative cost? | No. When a Character is moved from one area to another, it loses the effects it was subject to in the original area. |
| Does 'with a type including "XX"' include cards with types such as "Former XX" and "XX Allies"? | Yes, it does. |
| Can I use a card with 0 cost if I have no DON!! cards in my cost area? | Yes, you can. |
| In the Refresh Phase, can I choose not to return a DON!! card and leave it as given to a card? | No, you must always return DON!! cards to the cost area. |
| What happens if the attacking or defending Character is moved to a different area during Attack/Block/Counter Step? | At the end of each step, if that Character has moved to another area, the battle ends without proceeding to the next step. |
| If a Character with −2000 power attacks a Character with −2000 power, who wins? | The attacker wins. The attacked Character is K.O.'d. If the target was a Leader, 1 damage is dealt. |
| I won a battle using a Character with 0 power against a Character with 0 power. Is my opponent's Character K.O.'d? | Yes. |
| If an effect states "up to" a specified number of cards, can I choose 0 cards? | Yes, you can choose 0 cards. |
| Can I activate the effect of a card with a specified target if that target is not present? | Yes, but nothing will happen in that case. |
| Where a "during this turn" effect is activated by an [Activate: Main] effect, is it still valid if the activating card moves to another area? | Yes, it is valid. |
| When a Life card added to hand during damage has a [Trigger], and I activate the Trigger instead, will "when you activate an Event" effects fire? | No, they will not. |
| When text refers to 1 card from the top or bottom of Life cards, what card is it? | The card at the very top or very bottom of the Life area. |
| When directed to place a card at the top or bottom of Life cards, where do I place it? | At the very top or very bottom. If the Life area is empty, place it there. Cards are placed face-down unless directed otherwise. |
| When I add a card from my deck to my Life cards, can I or my opponent look at it? | No. |
| Can I activate the Trigger of a card placed face-up in my Life area? | Yes. |

## Keywords

| Question | Answer |
|---|---|
| Can a [DON!! x○] effect still be activated if the card has more than the specified number of DON!! cards? | Yes, the effect can still be activated. |
| When returning DON!! cards according to a [DON!! −○:] effect, can I return DON!! cards already given to Characters or Leader? | Yes. You can return any DON!! cards from your Leader area, Stage area, Character area, or cost area. |
| Can I activate Counters of Characters on the field like [Counter +1000]? | No, you can only activate the Counters of cards in your hand. |
| Can I use a Counter to increase the power of a card not being attacked? | Yes. However, the effect will end at the end of the current battle. |
| Do I need to pay a cost to use a Character's Counter? | No, there is no cost for using a Character card's Counter. |
| Can I activate an Event card's [Counter] effect during my Main Phase? | No. You can only activate it during the Counter Step of a battle in which your opponent has attacked. |
| If I have 5 Characters and play another Character, when I trash one, will its [On K.O.] effect activate? | No. The trashed Character is not K.O.'d, but directly moved to trash. |
| Does [On K.O.] activate when the card with the effect is K.O.'d, or when it K.O.'s an opponent's Character? | It activates when the card with the effect is K.O.'d. Not when it K.O.'s an opponent's Character. |
| If there are cards of the same name with [Once Per Turn] effects, can I activate each of their effects during the same turn? | Yes. |
| Can I play a Character card with an [On Play] effect without activating the effect? | No, you must activate it whenever possible. However, if the effect has a cost, you may choose not to pay it and play the card without activating the effect. |
| Does [When Attacking] activate when another card without this effect attacks? | No. It only activates when the card with this effect attacks. |

## Keyword Effects

| Question | Answer |
|---|---|
| Can I use 2 or more [Blocker] effects in a single battle? | No. During a single Block Step, you can activate up to 1 [Blocker] effect. |
| Can I activate the [Blocker] of a Character being attacked? | No. |
| When I activate a card's [Blocker], can I use a Counter to increase that card's power? | Yes. |
| When my opponent is attacking, if I have a [Blocker] Character, do I have to activate that [Blocker]? | No, you can choose not to activate a [Blocker]. |
| Can I activate the [Blocker] of a rested Character? | No. |
| Can I choose not to trash a Life card if I deal damage using a Character with [Banish]? | No. |
| Can I choose to deal 1 damage if I deal damage using a Character with [Double Attack]? | No. |
| When dealing 2 damage with [Double Attack], if this Character loses [Double Attack] or leaves the field due to a Trigger effect, is the remaining damage still dealt? | Yes. Since the damage to be dealt is fixed at 2, it will be dealt even if the Character leaves the field or loses [Double Attack]. |
| If my opponent has 1 Life card, can I win by using [Double Attack] to deal 2 damage? | No. |
| Can I add a card to my hand without activating its [Trigger]? | Yes. |
| When I move a Life card due to an effect like "Add 1 card from the top of your Life cards to your hand", can I activate its [Trigger]? | No. [Trigger] effects can only be activated upon taking damage. |
| I have taken 2 damage from a [Double Attack]. If the first damage card has a [Trigger], do I activate it before the second damage? | Yes. The [Trigger] is activated before the second damage. If you still have 1 or more Life cards after the first Trigger, check a Life card for the second damage. |
| When I activate [OP03-074 Top Knot]'s [Trigger] "Activate this card's [Main] effect", can I skip its "DON!! −2" activation cost? | No. |
| Which activates first: the [When Attacking] effect of the attacker, or my [On Your Opponent's Attack] effect? | Your opponent's [When Attacking] effect activates first. |
| If I have a [On Your Opponent's Attack] card on my field and my opponent targets a different card, can I still activate it? | Yes. |

## Advanced Rules

| Question | Answer |
|---|---|
| "Look at up to 1 card from the top of your or your opponent's Life cards, and place it at the top or bottom of the Life cards." — Can I look at my Life cards and place it at my opponent's? | No. After looking, place it at the top or bottom of that card's owner's Life cards. |
| Can DON!! cards given to Characters, Leaders, or in the DON!! deck be set as active or rested by effects? | No, they cannot. |
| Do effects that read "cannot be rested" mean that the affected Characters cannot attack? | Yes. These Characters cannot perform any actions that require resting, such as attacking or activating [Blocker]. |
| Do "cannot be rested" Characters lose the ability to activate [Blocker]? | Yes. |
| Can "cannot be rested" Characters be rested by effects of other cards? | No. |
| If a Leader has the effect "treated as a card with all card names, types, and attributes", can it fulfill a condition "If your Leader has the {XX} type"? | Yes. |
| If a Leader has the "all card names, types, and attributes" effect, can it K.O. a Character that "cannot be K.O.'d by <Slash> attribute cards" in battle? | No. |
| "You may rest 1 of your DON!! cards:" — is this equivalent to "➀ (You may rest the specified number of DON!! cards in your cost area.):"? | Yes, both refer to activation costs paid by resting active DON!! cards in your cost area. |
