# ST21 — Card-Specific Q&A

| Card No. | Card Name | Question | Answer |
|---|---|---|---|
| ST21-003 | Sanji | After the Character selected by this [On Play] effect finishes attacking, if another Character or Leader attacks during the same turn, can my opponent activate [Blocker]? | Yes, they can. |
| ST21-015 | Roronoa Zoro | Can I use this [On K.O.] effect to play other [Roronoa Zoro] cards, such as "OP01-025 Roronoa Zoro"? | No, you cannot. The effect only plays cards with the exact name "Roronoa Zoro". |
| ST21-015 | Roronoa Zoro | During my opponent's turn, I played "OP09-013 Yasopp" using this [On K.O.] effect, and activated its "[On Play] Up to 1 of your Leader gains +1000 power until the end of your opponent's next turn." In this case, will my Leader gain +1000 power until the end of the current turn? | Yes, it will gain +1000 power until the end of the current turn. "Until the end of your opponent's next turn" refers to the end of your opponent's current turn. |
| ST21-017 | Gum-Gum Mole Pistol | When activating this [Main] effect, can I choose to give −5000 power to one Character, and then choose to K.O. a different Character with 2000 power or less? | Yes, you can. |

## Key Rulings Summary

- **ST21-003 Sanji** — Forces a chosen character (≤6000 power) to attack immediately during your Main Phase. After that forced attack, your opponent CAN still use [Blocker] on subsequent attacks in the same turn.
- **ST21-015 Roronoa Zoro** — The [On K.O.] effect only plays cards with the *exact* name "Roronoa Zoro". Cards from other sets with that name (e.g. OP01-025) cannot be played — only cards named exactly "Roronoa Zoro".
- **ST21-017 Gum-Gum Mole Pistol** — The −5000 power and the KO can target two *different* Characters. You give −5000 to one, then choose to KO another that has ≤2000 power (the −5000 applied to another character does not count).
